Key Risks & Mitigation

1. Non-Additionality & Leakage

Focus on documented waste streams (cotton stalks) with no competing use to prove climate benefit.

2. Inconsistent Quality

Implement a central quality control hub with lab testing and a digital tracking system to ensure standards.

3. High MRV Costs

Utilize digital MRV tools and aggregate small batches to make third-party audits economically viable.
